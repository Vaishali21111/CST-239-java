The gun and the bomb classes are child classes for the abstract class weapon 
when calling the method fireweapon(), the fireweapon method implemented in weapon class 
will be executed. 

//overriding 
The same method was defined again in the child classes, this is called override. 
When Bomb call fireweapon() , the method defined in bomb will be executed . 
When Gun call fireweapon() , the method defined in gun will be executed .
